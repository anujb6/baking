Classic Chessecake Recipe

Look no further for a ultra smooth classic cheesecake recipe! Paired with buttery graham cracker crust, no one can deny its simple decandence.for better result bake it in a water bath.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

1 and 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham crackers)
5 tablespoons(70g) unslated butter,melted
1/4 cup(50g) granulated sugar

Chessecake

four 8-once blocks(904g) full-fat cream cheese.softened to room temprature
1 cup(200g) granulated sugar
1 cup(240g) full fat-sour cream at room temprature
1 pure teaspoon vanilla extract
2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice(optional, but recommended)
3 large eggs at room temprature
topping suggestions: salted caramel, lemon curd, strawberry topping, choclate ganache, red wine choclate ganache, fresh fruit, whipped cream or raspberry sauce(recipe in notes)

Instructions

Adjust the oven rack to the lower-middle position and preheat oven to 350 deg F(177degC)

Make the crust: Using the food processor, pulse the graham crackers into crumbs.pour into a medium bowl and stir in sugar and melted butter until combined.(You pulse it all together in food processor.) Mixture will be sandy.Press firmly into the bottom and slightly up the sides of a 9-inch or 10-inch springform pan.No need to grease the pan first.I use the bottom of a measuring cup to pack the crust down tightly.Pre-bake for 8 minutes.Remove from the oven and plcae the hot pan on a large piece of aluminium foil.The foil will wrap around the pan for water bath in step 4. allow crust to slightlycool as you prepare the filling

make the filling:Using a handheld or stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, beat the cream chease and granulated sugar together at a medium-high speed in a large bowl until the mixture is smooth and creamy, about 2 minutes.Add the sour cream,vanilla extractr,and lemon

Prepare the simple water bath(see note):Boil a pot of water.You need 1 inch of water in your roasting pan for the water bath, so make sure you boil enough. I use the entire kettle of hot water.As the water is heating up,wrap the aluminium foil aroung the springform pan.Pour the chesseckae batter ont he top off the crust. Use a rubber spatula or spoon to smootjh it into an even layer. Place the pan inside of a large roasting pan.carefully pour the hot water inside of the pan and place in the oven.

bake the chessecake for 55-70 minutes or until the center is almost set. when it's done, the centre of the cheesecake sit in the oven in the water bath as it cools down for 1 hour.Remove from the oven and water bath as it cools down for 1 hour.Remove from the oven and water bath, then cool chessecake completely at room temprature.Then refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 24 hours or overnight.

Serve chessecake with desired toppings.Cover and store leftover chessecake in the refrigerator for up to 5 days
